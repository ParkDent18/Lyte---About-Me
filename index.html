About Me...

I'm someone who finds joy in the simple, meaningful things that ground me in life. Music flows through everything I do it lifts my spirit, soothes my soul, and connects me to something deeper. My faith in God is the foundation of who I am, guiding my choices and giving me purpose each day. When I'm not listening to music or spending time in worship, you'll likely find me out in the early morning chill, waiting in the blind for waterfowl. There's something sacred about those quiet moments in nature watching the sunrise paint the sky, hearing the whistle of wings overhead, and feeling grateful for the beauty of creation all around me.
